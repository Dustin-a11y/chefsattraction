Our Story

From the kitchen
to your skin.

I've spent my career working with the finest ingredients. Tallow wasn't new to me — it was essential.

As a professional chef, I've always known the power of rendered animal fats. They nourish, protect, and heal. One day, I realized: if tallow can transform food, what can it do for skin?

The answer changed everything. Glazed & Infused was born from that moment — a line of skincare products that bridges the kitchen and the vanity, using the same premium ingredients and meticulous attention to detail that goes into every dish I create.

No fillers. No synthetic chemicals. Just centuries of kitchen wisdom, bottled.
